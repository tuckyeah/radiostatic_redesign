Maybe June is right, and the old man is crazy.

But that would make me crazy too.

Then again, maybe I already am.

I found something among his journals. Something that doesn't make any sense.

He talks about me, Charlie Waits, coming to the bar and befriending him.

He even included notes from me, and I recognized my own handwriting.

At first I didn't know what to think - none of it made sense.

But I can feel that it's real, that he's right. I can feel it in my bones, in the way you can feel the laws of gravity operating upon you.

... I did not like what I read.
