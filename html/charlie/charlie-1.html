I still remember the first time I met June. It was in a dream.

searching for a hidden treasure of some kind.

I was standing in my kitchen (but it wasn't my kitchen), reading a map on a placemat on the table. She came over and asked if there was any room for her.

I stepped aside and she stood next to me, her shoulder against mine, and my heart started pounding.

I guess it hasn't really stopped since.

I started looking forward to going to sleep every night, praying that we would meet up again.

I don't know pretend to have an explanation, but she started showing up in my dreams regularly, and we've been voyaging together ever since.

Truth be told, I can't remember, and that scares me.

I'm scared of a lot of things.

I'm not sure if I'm awake or dreaming anymore. I'm not sure if this is actually reality, or if I'm just unable to wake up.

I can't tell her though. It would be unprofessional.
