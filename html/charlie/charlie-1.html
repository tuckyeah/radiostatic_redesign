I still remember the first time I met June. It was in a dream and we were searching for a hidden treasure of some kind.

I was standing in my kitchen (but it wasn't my kitchen), reading a map on a placemat on the table. She came over and asked if there was any room for her.

I stepped aside and she stood next to me, her shoulder against mine, and my heart started pounding.

I guess it hasn't really stopped since.

I started looking forward to going to sleep every night, praying that we would meet up again.

Now, I'm never sure when I'm awake or when I'm dreaming.

But isn't that also supposed to be a side effect of falling in love?

This is the longest we've ever spent together. I've lost track of time. If I fell asleep, I don't remember when. I keep looking at my hands to try and wake myself up, but they seem just as real as I do.

I can't tell her any of this. It would be unprofessional.

And besides, what if I'm wrong?

What if she's real,

and I'm just crazy?
