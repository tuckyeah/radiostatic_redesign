We woke up shortly after sunrise and began the long trek into the desert.

There we found what I believe Leopold was referring to.

A sea of satellites, stretching all the way out into the mountains. By the looks of things, they are abandoned and long forgotten.

Still, there is a hum of energy in the air - not electricity, but something else.

"Is this what he was talking about?" I asked. "This is where his son is?"

Charlie hesitated. "Let's look around. I just... I can feel something. I can't explain it."
