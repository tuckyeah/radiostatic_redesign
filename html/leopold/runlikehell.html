Hearing his voice gave me the courage I needed, and after a time, I was able to gather my strength and the majority of my wits about me. If there was only thing to do now, I would not let their deaths be in vain.

I buried my beloved in the reams of our research, reading and savoring each page we had written before gently adding it to the growing pile in the middle of the house.



After allowing myself a moment to marvel at the sheer size and breadth of our work,

I doused it in gasoline...

and watched it burn.
