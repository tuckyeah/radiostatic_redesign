MY DEAR--

I knew it! I knew this was the perfect place, that my OUR diligence would pay off! Yesterday eve, on my Daily Constitutional into the dunes, I came across it: a sea of satellites stretching as far as the eye can see, disappearing into the distant mountains! Oh, my love - you cannot imagine the immense energy there.

I am in the process of Planning a Trip out there soon, which I will of course document to you in its entirely. He is here, I can feel it. And I hope with every ounce of my being I am right.

But I must confess, I am plagued by doubts, pursued by insecurities, hunted by the relentless Wolves forever nipping at my heels.

It is just that I am longing something fierce for your voice and your touch. There is just so much I wish I could tell you, so much for which I would apologize and yet, I find my words are failing me.


Please do not let this be another Mistake in a line of so many.


Please do not let me be led Astray.


Dreaming of you,
Leopold
